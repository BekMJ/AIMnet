# AIMNet Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 6, 2026

AIMNet is a Bluetooth sensor monitoring app from NexluSense. The app connects to compatible AIMNet Bluetooth Low Energy sensors and displays methane and H2S telemetry on your iPhone or iPad.

## Data We Collect

AIMNet does not collect personal information, create user accounts, or send sensor readings to NexluSense servers.

Sensor readings are processed on your device. Exported CSV or JSON files are shared only when you choose to use the iOS share sheet, such as AirDrop, Mail, Gmail, Drive, or another app you select.

## Bluetooth

AIMNet uses Bluetooth to discover and connect to nearby compatible AIMNet sensors. Bluetooth access is used only for sensor discovery, connection, and telemetry reading.

## Local Data

The app may store recent sensor sessions locally on your device so you can review or export readings. Local data remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

## Third-Party Services

AIMNet does not include third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

If you export data using another app or service, that export is handled by the app or service you select and is subject to that provider's privacy policy.
