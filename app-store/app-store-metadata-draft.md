## Description

AIMNet connects to compatible AIMNet Bluetooth Low Energy gas sensors and displays live methane and H2S telemetry on iPhone and iPad.

Use AIMNet to scan for nearby AIMNet sensors, connect over Bluetooth, monitor live readings, review connection status, and share recorded session data as CSV or JSON through the standard iOS share sheet.

Features:

- Bluetooth scanning for compatible AIMNet sensors
- Live methane and H2S telemetry display
- Connection status and detected-device information
- Session recording while connected
- CSV and JSON sharing through AirDrop, Mail, Drive, and other installed apps
- iPhone and iPad support

AIMNet requires compatible AIMNet BLE sensor hardware.

## Review Notes

AIMNet requires compatible AIMNet Bluetooth Low Energy methane/H2S sensor hardware.

The app scans for nearby BLE peripherals whose advertised name begins with "AIMNet".

Testing with hardware:

1. Turn on Bluetooth.
2. Power on an AIMNet sensor near the iPhone or iPad.
3. Open AIMNet.
4. Tap "Scan Bluetooth Sensors".
5. Select the discovered AIMNet sensor.
6. Live telemetry appears automatically after connection.
7. Use "Share CSV" or "Share JSON" to export a session through the iOS share sheet.

No login is required. Sensor readings are processed locally on device. CSV and JSON files are shared only when the user explicitly chooses a share destination.

If physical hardware is not available to the review team, provide a demo mode or arrange hardware access before submission.
