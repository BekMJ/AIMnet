# App Privacy Answers Draft

Use these answers only if the app remains as currently implemented: no login, no analytics, no advertising, no server upload, and exports only through user-initiated sharing.

## Data Collection

Does this app collect data from this app?

Answer: No

Rationale: Sensor readings are processed locally on device. The app does not transmit data to NexluSense servers. User-initiated CSV/JSON sharing through the iOS share sheet is controlled by the user and the selected destination app.

## Tracking

Does this app use data for tracking purposes?

Answer: No

## Third-Party Advertising

Answer: No

## Analytics

Answer: No

## Account Information

Answer: No account creation or login.

## Location

Answer: No precise or coarse location collection, unless a future version adds location-tagged samples.

## Health Data

Answer: No, unless the app is later positioned as collecting regulated health data.

## Important Caveat

If a future version adds cloud sync, analytics, crash reporting with identifiers, user accounts, location tagging, email collection, or server-side storage, these answers must be updated in App Store Connect.
